NOTES

This is my website.

And It works. It loads instantly, it’s readable, and It doesn’t beg for attention, validation, or engagement.

Already better than most things you can find on the internet, right?

If you came here expecting a polished portfolio, a cinematic introduction, or a perfectly branded version of me, you’re in the wrong fucking place. This page refuses to perform for you, it forces you to engage or leave.

You are overestimating appearances.

Most people see two photos, a bunch of cool outfits and a clean LinkedIn profile and immediately build a fantasy character: the “cool guy”, the “put-together kid”, the “half-genius”, the “Prince Charming with a skincare routine”.

Accuracy? 17.38% at best.

Because remember, a surface isn’t a person and a vibe isn’t a personality.

Why this looks like this

This page is intentionally plain so your brain has to do the work: pay attention, think, and try to fill the gaps. If you can’t engage with text without visual fireworks, well, consider taking a break from Instagram and TikTok.

If you want to understand someone, you need effort, attention and real conversations, not three filtered photos and a quote stolen from a dead philosopher you never read.
